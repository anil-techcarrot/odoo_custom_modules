# TechCarrot CRM Customization MLR

## Overview
The **TechCarrot CRM Customization MLR** module is an advanced enhancement to Odoo's standard CRM functionality, specifically designed to address the sophisticated customer relationship management needs of TechCarrot. This module extends the CRM system with specialized fields, master data management, and intelligent automation to provide comprehensive opportunity tracking and enhanced sales process management.

## Key Features

### Enhanced Lead and Opportunity Management
- **Point of Contact Integration**: Advanced contact person assignment with automatic population from customer records
- **Deal Manager Assignment**: Employee-based deal management with automatic user assignment synchronization
- **Practice Area Classification**: Structured practice area categorization for service-based business models
- **Industry Specialization**: Comprehensive industry classification system for market segmentation
- **Business Type Categorization**: Flexible business type classification for opportunity categorization

### Advanced Date Tracking
- **Proposal Submission Tracking**: Dual date tracking for client proposals and internal proposal management
- **Timeline Management**: Enhanced timeline management for proposal and opportunity lifecycle
- **Milestone Tracking**: Key milestone tracking throughout the sales process
- **Deadline Management**: Comprehensive deadline management for proposal-based sales
- **Historical Analysis**: Historical date analysis for sales process optimization

### Presales Integration
- **Presales Engagement Tracking**: Boolean flag for presales team involvement
- **Resource Planning**: Enhanced resource planning through presales engagement indicators
- **Team Collaboration**: Improved collaboration between sales and presales teams
- **Process Optimization**: Streamlined processes based on presales involvement
- **Performance Analytics**: Analytics on presales engagement and conversion rates

### Master Data Management
- **Practice Master**: Centralized practice area management with codes and descriptions
- **Industry Master**: Comprehensive industry classification with unique constraints
- **Lead Type Master**: Flexible lead type management for opportunity classification
- **Data Consistency**: Enforced data consistency through master data controls
- **Reference Data**: Standardized reference data across all CRM processes
