Odoo Enterprise Payroll Multi-Currency

Note: This app is only compatible with Odoo Enterprise.

Simplify global payroll management with seamless multi-currency support. Process payslips, contracts, and salary attachments in any currency, automate conversions using rates of the slip accounting date, and ensure accurate journal entries in contract currency. Fully compatible with payroll batches, refunds, and Odoo’s accounting workflows—designed for error-free, compliant payroll across borders.

Features
Multi-Currency Payslips

Generate payslips in various currencies, catering to a diverse workforce.

Multi-Currency Contracts

Handle salary attachments in employee current contract currency

Multi-Currency Attachments

Handle salary attachments in various currencies with automatic conversion.

Accounting Integration

Seamlessly integrate with accounting for accurate financial records.

Refunds & Reversals

Easily process payslip refunds and reversals with multi-currency support.

Contract-Centric Entries

Generate journal entries based on the contract currency for consistency.

Payroll Reports

Generate payslip reports with multi-currency
